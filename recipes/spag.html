Spaghetti Aglio e Olio Recipe

Description

Ingredients (Serves 2)

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt (to taste)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente (about 10 minutes). Reserve 1/4 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add sliced garlic and chili flakes (if using), and saut√© until the garlic is golden (not burnt).
  3. Add the drained spaghetti and reserved pasta water to the pan. Toss to coat evenly in the garlic oil.
  4. Season with salt and sprinkle parsley on top (if using). Serve hot.
